### 分析词义

"Mounted" 是 **mount** 的过去分词。常见义：**安装固定**（**mounted camera**）、**骑乘**（**mounted the horse**）、**发起进攻**（**mounted an attack**）、**裱挂**（**mounted photograph**）。

<Pronunciation word="Mounted" uk="[ˈmaʊntɪd]" us="[ˈmaʊntɪd]" client:only />

### 列举例句

1. "A security camera was mounted above the door." (门上方安装了一盏监控摄像头。)
2. "The soldiers mounted their horses at dawn." (士兵黎明时上马。)
3. "The museum mounted a new exhibition on ancient coins." (博物馆新办了一场古钱币展览。)

### 词根分析

**mount** 来自拉丁语 **mons/mont-**（山），引申为“登上、爬上、固定到支架上”。

### 词缀分析

**mount** + **-ed**；名词 **mountain** 与 **mount** 同源。

### 文化背景

安防与摄影常说 **wall-mounted**；军事史 **mounted troops** 指骑兵；艺术 **mounted print** 指裱框作品。

### 单词变形

- 固定搭配: "wall-mounted" (壁挂式), "mounted police" (骑警), "mounted attack" (发动进攻)

### 记忆辅助

**mount**（登上）+ **-ed** → 已经 **装上去或骑上去** 的状态。

### 助记故事

"After the sensor was mounted on the wall, mounted officers passed below during the mounted parade rehearsal." (传感器装到墙上后，骑警从下方经过，那是骑兵游行彩排。)
