### 分析词义

"Explained" 表示已经给出说明或理由，如 "The teacher explained the rule clearly."（老师把规则讲清楚了。）形容词用法中 "well explained" 指解释到位；"easily explained" 指原因简单明了。

<Pronunciation word="Explained" uk="/ɪkˈspleɪnd/" us="/ɪkˈspleɪnd/" client:only />

### 列举例句

1. "She explained why the meeting was postponed." (她说明了会议为何延期。)
2. "The diagram explained the process step by step." (图示逐步解释了流程。)
3. "His absence was explained by a medical note." (他的缺席有医疗证明作说明。)

### 词根分析

"Explain" 来自拉丁语 explanare，由 ex-（出）与 planus（平坦）构成，原义“摊平、阐明”。

### 词缀分析

ex-（向外）+ plan（平）→ explain + -ed；名词 explanation（解释）。

### 文化背景

学术写作要求 phenomena be explained, not merely described；客服邮件常用 "as explained below" 引导读者查看细节。

### 单词变形

- 原形: explain
- 现在分词: explaining
- 名词: explanation
- 固定搭配: "explained away" (搪塞过去), "self-explained" (不言自明), "explain oneself" (说明自己的立场)

### 记忆辅助

ex- 拿出来 + plan 摊平 → explain 讲清楚 → explained 已经讲明白了。

### 助记故事

"The nurse explained how to take the medicine before the patient left the clinic." (患者离开诊所前，护士说明了如何服药。)
