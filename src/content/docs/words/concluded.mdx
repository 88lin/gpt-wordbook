### 分析词义

活动收尾（concluded the meeting）、研究得出判断（concluded that the drug is safe）、合同缔结（concluded an agreement）。语气偏正式。

### 列举例句

1. "The panel concluded that more data were needed." (专家组断定还需要更多数据。)
2. "She concluded her speech with a thank-you to volunteers." (她以感谢志愿者结束了演讲。)
3. "They concluded a trade deal after months of talks." (数月谈判后，他们达成贸易协议。)

### 词根分析

拉丁语 concludere（关闭、终结），con- + claudere（关闭），与 include、exclude 中的 clud 同源。

### 词缀分析

con-（完全）+ clud（关）+ -ed；名词 conclusion。

### 文化背景

学术论文 The authors concluded... 是标准表述；会议 concluded successfully 常见于新闻稿。与 close 相比 conclude 更书面。

### 单词变形

- 固定搭配: "concluded that" (断定), "concluded with" (以…结束), "concluded agreement" (达成协议)
- 名词: conclusion

### 记忆辅助

include 是关在里面，conclude 是“完全关上”——讨论关上时就得出结论。

### 助记故事

"After reviewing the logs, the engineer concluded the outage started at 2:07 a.m." (查阅日志后，工程师断定故障始于凌晨2点07分。)
