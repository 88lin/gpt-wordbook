### 分析词义

**Manifestly** 是形容词 **manifest** 的副词形式。它用于强调某事是非常明显的、不容置疑的，通常用于正式的论述或批评中。

<Pronunciation word="manifestly" uk="/ˈmæn.ɪ.fɛst.li/" us="/ˈmæn.ɪ.fɛst.li/" client:only />

### 列举例句

1. "The new law is **manifestly** unfair to low-income families." (新法律显然对低收入家庭不公平。)
2. "He was **manifestly** unfit for such a demanding job." (他显然不适合这份要求如此之高的工作。)
3. "The evidence provided was **manifestly** false." (提供的证据显然是虚假的。)

### 词根分析

- **manifest**: 明显的。
- **-ly**: 副词后缀。

### 词缀分析

- **后缀**: **-ly**。
- 同根词: **manifest** (明显的), **manifestation** (表现)。

### 文化背景

在法律术语中，`manifestly` 常用于形容判决或行为严重违背了常识或公平原则（如 "manifestly unreasonable"）。它比简单的 "clearly" 更有力、更具批判性。

### 单词变形

- 相关形容词: **manifest**

### 记忆辅助

**Manifest**（明显的）+ **-ly**（地）= 显然地。想象一张摆在桌子上的海报，每个人都能看清楚上面的字。

### 助记故事

The king claimed he was a simple man who loved his people. However, he lived in a palace of gold while his subjects were hungry. His life was **manifestly** different from his words. The people didn't need a philosopher to tell them the truth; it was **manifestly** clear every time they looked at the golden gates.
