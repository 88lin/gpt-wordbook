### 分析词义

**Martial** 指与战争、军队或战士有关的事物。它通常带有“纪律严明”或“威武”的联想。

<Pronunciation word="martial" uk="/ˈmɑːʃl/" us="/ˈmɑːrʃl/" client:only />

### 列举例句

1. "The government declared **martial** law to restore order in the city." (政府宣布实行军事管制/戒严以恢复城市秩序。)
2. "He is an expert in various **martial** arts, including karate and judo." (他是各种武术的专家，包括空手道和柔道。)
3. "The soldiers marched with a **martial** bearing." (士兵们以威武的军姿行进。)

### 词根分析

- **marti-**: 源自拉丁语 *Mars* (马尔斯)，古罗马神话中的战神。
- **-al**: 形容词后缀。

### 词缀分析

- **后缀**: **-al**。
- 同根词: **Mars** (火星/战神), **Martian** (火星人)。

### 文化背景

最重要的搭配是 **Martial arts**（武术）。在法律上，**Martial law**（戒严令/军法）是指在紧急情况下，军队取代民事政府行使权力。

### 单词变形

- 副词形式: **martially**

### 记忆辅助

联想 **Mars**（战神）。一个 **martial** 的东西就是属于“战神”的，即与军事和武力有关。

### 助记故事

The young prince was tired of books; he wanted to learn **martial** skills. He wanted to ride horses and lead armies. His father, the king, said, "True **martial** spirit is not just about fighting; it's about the discipline to protect those who cannot fight for themselves." The prince remembered this as he practiced his **martial** arts every dawn.
