### 分析词义

**Marrow** 有三个主要含义：
1. **解剖学**: **Bone marrow**（骨髓），指骨骼中心产生血细胞的软组织。
2. **植物学**: （英式英语）指一种大型的、绿皮的西葫芦类蔬菜。
3. **隐喻**: 指某事物的最核心、最本质的部分（如 the marrow of the argument）。

### 列举例句

1. "The patient needs a bone **marrow** transplant to treat the leukemia." (患者需要骨髓移植来治疗白血病。)
2. "We harvested a giant **marrow** from the garden for Sunday dinner." (我们从花园里采摘了一个巨大的嫩葫芦做周日晚餐。)
3. "His speech went straight to the **marrow** of the issue." (他的演讲直指问题的核心。)

### 词根分析

- 源自古英语 *mearh*，意为“髓”或“精髓”。

### 词缀分析

无明显前缀或后缀。

### 文化背景

短语 "to the marrow" 意为“彻骨地、深入地”（如 frozen to the marrow 冻到骨子里）。在英国园艺文化中，种出最大的 `marrow`（大嫩葫芦）常是乡村竞赛的项目。

### 单词变形

- 复数形式: **marrows**

The old gardener grew a **marrow** that was as big as a sleeping dog. He entered it into the village competition. While others talked about the size, he cut it open to show the rich, soft inside. "The outside is for show," he said, "but the **marrow** is for the soul." He won the gold medal for having the best heart and the best **marrow**.
