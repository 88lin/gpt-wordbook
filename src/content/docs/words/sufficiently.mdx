### 分析词义

常用于正式或书面语境，表示数量或质量“够用”；法律、技术文档里常见 sufficiently clear、reasonably and sufficiently。

<Pronunciation word="Sufficiently" uk="[səˈfɪʃntli]" us="[səˈfɪʃntli]" client:only />

### 列举例句

1. "The room was not sufficiently heated for newborn chicks." (房间对雏鸡来说不够暖和。)
2. "Applicants must be sufficiently fluent to handle client calls." (申请人须足够流利，能应付客户来电。)
3. "The evidence was not sufficiently strong to secure a conviction." (证据不够有力，不足以定罪。)

### 词根分析

拉丁语 sufficere（在下面供给、补足），sub- + facere（做）；sufficient + -ly。

### 词缀分析

形容词 sufficient + 副词后缀 "-ly"。

### 文化背景

学术评审、合规审计常用“是否 sufficiently documented”；口语可换 enough 更自然。

### 单词变形

- 固定搭配: "sufficiently large" (足够大), "sufficiently warned" (已充分警示)
- 形容词: sufficient；名词: sufficiency

### 记忆辅助

sufficient（足够的）+ ly → 达到“够线”的程度。

### 助记故事

"He had not slept sufficiently before the exam, so he felt foggy." (考试前他睡眠不够，所以头脑发昏。)
